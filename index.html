Who are we?

At b-12 we are building AI agents to facilitate the use of automated labs

Why is this a problem?

Automated labs are the future of chemistry and lab experimentation. They are far more reliable, reproducible, and ultimately more efficient than traditional labs. However, **they are typically hard to use**. And usually come with a steep learning curve.
